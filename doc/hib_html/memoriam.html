This version of Hibridon is dedicated to the memory of Sheldon Green and Pierre Valiron whose untimely deaths in 1995 and 2009 robbed us of two stellar scientists who made long-lasting and invaluable contributions to the field of inelastic molecular collisions. Ave atque vale.
